Planet Polluter

Project Description

This project was created at the 2018 NASA SpaceApps Hackathon in Boston, where we took 1st place. This was created by myself Christian Kuss, Brock Fenbert, Blake McHale, and Ryan Lung. The game is modeled after Plague Inc. where the goal is to infect the entire world with a virus. In our game, the goal is to pollute the entire world by placing factories, farms and energy plants.

Here are my team-member's websites:
Christian Kuss: christiankuss.com
Blake McHale: blakemchale.com

Project Details

  • Used Unity and C#

  • Incorporated global NASA CO2 and temperature data as parameters for the simulation

  • Very basic prototype that could become more complex with time
